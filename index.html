I am Novia

I am a product designer at NowPow, creating healthcare technology to facilitate whole-person care, with a focus on social needs. My research and design interests include patient-provider collaboration and self-management technology for children and youth with mental/behavioral health needs and other chronic illnesses.

A Researcher

I hold a MS in Information from University of Michigan, with a focus on UX Research/Design and HCI, and a BS in Psychology from San Jose State University.

Child-Centered Intervention

Leverage a child’s role as the natural focus of their care team to help bridge communication gaps and facilitate collaboration.

Memory and Password Security

Investigate password composition requirements’ effects on users’ recall ability, self-predicted memorability, and perceived security.

Cybersecurity & Human Factors

A NSF funded study focusing on understanding human cognition in cyber network defense and human decision making models.
