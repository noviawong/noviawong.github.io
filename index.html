Nice to meet you! I am Novia.

I am a third year Informatics PhD student at University of California Irvine, advised by Dr. Madhu Reddy . I study and design digital tools to support collaborative mental health management. My research aims to support adolescents and families, but I have also worked with other populations like older adults and young adults.

Leveraging my background in psychology and product design, I take a user-centric/HCI approach in my research to create engaging, culturally relevant, and need-appropriate digital mental health tools.

I'm a researcher.

I hold a MS from University of Michigan in Information Science, specializing in UX Research/Design and HCI, and a BS from San Jose State University in Psychology. Some of my research highlights:

Understanding and Evaluating Cultural Relevannce of Digital Mental Health Tools

Working with the Asian American adolescent and young adult populations, we conduct focus groups, digital ethnographic research, and participatory design workshops to identify design requirements for creating culturally relevant digital mental health tools.

The process includes need-elicitation, participatory design sessions, prototype and software development, and clinical trials. This research is a part of Northwestern University's Center for Behavioral Intervention Technologies (CBITs) and funded by NIMH.

Evaluating Cyber Security and User Experience of Effective Password Creation

We conducted interviews, mind-mapping, sysemtatic review, and a survey-based experiment to investgate various aspects of cyber security, including password composition requirements’ effects on users’ recall ability, self-predicted memorability, and perceived security.

I'm also a UX designer.

My process involves identifying user needs and iterating design solutions through mixed method user research. I collaborate with product managers and developers to create high fidelity interactive protoypes using Adobe XD and Figma.

NowPow: PowRx, NowRx, CommRx, FindRx

Researched and designed referral webapps for care providers, case managers, and patients. Referrals focus on social needs.

TechSmith: Hotkeys and Favorites

Designed new functionalities for a video editing desktop application, Camtasia, including hotkeys and favorites.

Bariatric Surgery Decision Aid

Researched and designed a decision aid with educational materials on post-surgery life style changes for bariatric patients.
